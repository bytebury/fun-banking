How it Works

Fun Banking provides a comprehensive and engaging approach to financial education. By creating virtual banks, managing transactions, and gaining insights into learners' financial journeys, Fun Banking equips families and organizations with a powerful tool to raise financially savvy individuals. With Fun Banking, learning about money management becomes interactive, practical, and fun!

Users create banks and they can add customers to those banks. Once you have customers, you can share the link to your bank to them and they can sign in using their PINs that you setup. The interface is easy to navigate on any device.

We're working on adding some guides to help new users out, but we recommend you play around first with some mock data before sharing the link out.
